# Coinvestopedia Product Marketing Context

## 🎯 Core Identity
Coinvestopedia is an institutional-grade cryptocurrency research and analytics platform. It provides high-signal intelligence for serious investors, bridging the gap between macro-economic data, on-chain metrics, and AI-driven ecosystem scoring.

## 🎨 Brand Guidelines (Technical)
- **Primary Color:** Emerald-500 (`#10B981`). Used for data visualization, primary actions, and brand highlights.
- **Background Palette (Dark):** 
  - Background: Deep Carbon (`#0A0A0A`)
  - Surface: Neutral-900 (`#171717`)
  - Border: Neutral-800 (`#303030`)
- **Background Palette (Light):**
  - Background: Light Metallic Grey (`#E0E2E6`)
  - Surface: Silver/White (`#F3F5F8`)
- **Typography:**
  - Headings: **Inter** (Technical, Modern, High-Legibility)
  - Body: **Source Sans 3** (Editorial, Clean, Research-focused)
- **Visual Stylings:**
  - **Leather Grain:** Subtle noise texture (0.08 - 0.15 opacity) for premium feel.
  - **Pattern Grid:** 40px grid with emerald-0.04 lines.
  - **Geometry:** Sharp edges (0px - 2px radius) for technical/luxury feel.
  - **Aesthetic:** Research Terminal, Clinical, High-Signal.

## 🗣️ Brand Voice
- **Tone:** Clinical, Authoritative, Quantitative, Analytical.
- **Avoid:** Retail "hype", crypto slang (HODL, LFG, moon), generic emojis (🚀, 🔥), over-promising, or emotional language.
- **Preferred Vocabulary:** Asymmetric risk, liquidity cycles, macro transmission, on-chain latency, correlation matrices, institutional positioning, ecosystem leverage.

## 💎 Value Proposition
"Bloomberg-level intelligence for the crypto ecosystem." We deliver the intelligence layer that serious capital uses to position, not just the candle charts that retail watches.
