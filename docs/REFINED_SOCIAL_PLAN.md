# Coinvestopedia Refined Social Media Strategy (Institutional v1.1)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This plan focuses on aligning all social content with the **Coinvestopedia Institutional Design System**. The aesthetic is "Research Terminal" (Bloomberg-style for Crypto) with a deep carbon and emerald palette.

## 🎨 Visual Identity Standard
- **Primary Color:** Emerald-500 (`#10B981`) for data and accents.
- **Backgrounds:** Deep Carbon (`#0A0A0A`) for high-contrast dark mode.
- **Surfaces:** Neutral-900 (`#171717`) with leather-grain texture.
- **Typography:** Inter (Headings) and Source Sans 3 (Body).
- **Layouts:** Sharp, geometric, asymmetric grid-based. No standard SaaS templates.

## 📝 Content Voice Refinement
- **Zero Hype:** Remove all retail slang (LFG, To the moon, etc.).
- **Data-First:** Lead with macro signals, on-chain metrics, and liquidity data.
- **Authoritative:** Speak to the portfolio manager and research analyst.

## 🚀 Execution Roadmap

### 1. Visual Asset Regeneration (High-Fidelity)
| Platform | Visual Concept | Style Parameters |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Instagram** | "The Liquidity Report" (Carousel) | Sharp text, grid backgrounds, leather-card charts. |
| **Twitter/X** | "On-Chain Pulse" (Single Impact) | Brutalist typography, high-contrast emerald signals. |
| **Pinterest** | "The Intelligence Stack" (Infographic) | Vertical narrative, technical diagrams, silver sheen. |
| **Medium** | "Macro Transmission" (Header) | Editorial grain texture, centered logo, Inter bold. |

### 2. Content Rewrite (Institutional Grade)
- **Instagram:** Focus on "Behavioral Archetypes" of institutional capital.
- **Medium:** Deep dive into "Macro-On-Chain Convergence".
- **Reddit:** Highly technical framework breakdowns (GEO Framework).
- **Twitter:** Threaded signals on "Smart Money Rotation".

## ✅ Success Criteria
- [ ] Visuals indistinguishable from the live app UI.
- [ ] Tone maintains "Clinical Authority".
- [ ] Brand logo integrated into the architecture of the images, not just slapped on.
