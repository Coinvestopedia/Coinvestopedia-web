### The literacy gap
Most investors open a chart and ask "where is price going?" Institutional desks open a terminal and ask "what is behavior telling us?" These are fundamentally different questions — and they produce fundamentally different results.

### The three signals that precede price

1. **Exchange flows** — net BTC moving onto exchanges signals sell pressure before it hits the order book.
2. **SOPR (Spent Output Profit Ratio)** — reveals whether the market is selling at profit or loss, identifying capitulation and accumulation zones.
3. **Miner reserve drawdowns** — when miners liquidate reserves, supply-side pressure builds; when they accumulate, conviction is high.

### The macro layer retail ignores
Crypto doesn't trade in isolation. Federal Reserve policy, equity index momentum, and USD strength directly influence digital asset flows. Cross-market correlation analysis is now table stakes for serious positioning.
