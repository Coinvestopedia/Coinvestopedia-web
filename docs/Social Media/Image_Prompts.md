# Social Media Image Prompts

Here are the prompts that can be used to generate the institutional-grade visual assets for social media platforms. These prompts are designed to yield the "Technical Research Terminal" aesthetic.

## Instagram
**Prompt:** A square composition showing a modern, high-end data terminal interface. The background should be a deep dark carbon (#0A0A0A) with a subtle leather-like grain texture. The UI features sharp geometric cards (0px to 2px border radius) displaying complex financial data, liquidity charts, and market behavior analytics. Accent colors should be exclusively a vibrant emerald green (#10B981) against the dark background. The overall aesthetic is institutional, clinical, authoritative, and data-first, avoiding any cartoonish elements or bright gradients.

## Twitter / X
**Prompt:** A horizontal landscape composition depicting a technical macro signal radar dashboard. The setting is a professional research terminal with a deep carbon background (#0A0A0A) featuring a very subtle grid pattern. The data visualization includes glowing emerald green (#10B981) data points, line charts, and metric callouts. The aesthetic is highly technical, high-signal, and quantitative, resembling a cutting-edge institutional trading terminal. Use minimalist, sharp design language with no excessive colors.

## Pinterest
**Prompt:** A vertical 9:16 infographic layout illustrating a multi-layered intelligence stack. The design features a dark carbon background (#0A0A0A) with silver-sheen glassmorphism panels. The structural elements are outlined and highlighted in crisp emerald green (#10B981). The layout is organized into clean, brutalist geometric sections representing different phases of market analysis. The typography should appear clinical and technical, and the overall look must be highly professional, structured, and institutional.
